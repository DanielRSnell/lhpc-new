# About Amalgamy

## Overview

Amalgamy is an AI/HPC orchestration platform that maximizes GPU revenue and ensures data sovereignty through contract-based orchestration software. Built for enterprises, neoclouds, OEMs, and government agencies, Amalgamy delivers 57-85% utilization gains while maintaining complete operational control.

## Core Value Proposition

High-performance orchestration that speeds time-to-result for AI, HPC, and digital twins under your complete control. Amalgamy eliminates vendor lock-in while providing enterprise-grade compute orchestration across any infrastructure.

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Bare-Metal Multi-Tenancy
Secure tenant isolation without virtualization overhead—onboard new tenants in days and maximize revenue per GPU with 57-85% higher utilization.

### 2. Workflow Portability
Write workflows once, deploy anywhere—on-premises clusters, neoclouds, sovereign clouds, or edge environments with consistent performance.

### 3. OEM-Agnostic Architecture
Works seamlessly across Supermicro, NVIDIA, AMD, Intel, Oracle, and AWS—future-proof your infrastructure without vendor lock-in.

### 4. Deep Observability
Real-time visibility into GPU utilization, job performance, and resource allocation with data-driven optimization recommendations.

## Target Markets

### Neoclouds (GPU Cloud Providers)
Independent infrastructure providers like CoreWeave and Lambda Labs use Amalgamy to maximize GPU revenue through efficient multi-tenant orchestration and rapid customer onboarding.

### OEM Partners
Hardware manufacturers including Supermicro, Dell, and HPE integrate Amalgamy to deliver turnkey AI/HPC solutions that differentiate their hardware with world-class orchestration software.

### Enterprises
Large organizations leverage Amalgamy for unified compute orchestration across fragmented infrastructure landscapes—hybrid cloud, on-premises, and edge deployments.

### Public Sector (Federal, Defense, SLED)
Government agencies achieve mission-critical computing with complete data sovereignty, StateRAMP compliance roadmap, and zero-trust security architecture.

### Research Institutions
Universities, national labs, and research centers democratize HPC access with self-service workflows, fair-share scheduling, and multi-tenant isolation.
